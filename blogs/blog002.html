Ever Looked at the Night Sky & Wondered

Main Article

Ever looked at the night sky & wondered,
how much mystery does its darkness holds..?

Ever you wondered how far the light of stars had travelled,
to finally stimulate few nerves of your eyes,
just to give you a sensation of blinking.

Ever wondered how would be the things
going on in the other part of the universe?

Ever looked at the night sky & wondered,
how amazing it would appear if we were able
to sense all the part of the electromagnetic spectrum,
not just only visible light spectrum region.

Ever you wondered what lies beyond the
darkness of our night sky?
Ever you wondered how all this came to an existence?

Ever you looked at the night sky & wondered,
how amazing would be the other star system of Milky Way?

Ever you wondered what would it feel like to travel
to some other planets?
How exciting it would be to feel the soil of other planets.

Ever looked at the night sky & wondered,
how amazing our universe is..

Ever you wondered whether all this have an end?

Ever you wondered how does all these workout so well?

Ever you looked at the night sky & wondered,
why is this night sky filling you with so much curiosity?

Ever you wondered why you are thinking of all this?

Ever you wondered how the same group of atoms
combined in one aspect, produces the screen at your hand,
when combined in other aspect, in other proportions,
produces one reading that screen.

Ever you looked at the night sky & wondered,
What to do? Whom to ask? How to know
the answer of all these questions & curiosities?

Ever you wondered who you really are?
The mind & its rational thoughts and awareness?
or The body & its temptations and emotions?
or The conscience & its instincts and moral values?
or The soul & its compassionate love and hate?
or Perhaps none of them, something totally different?
or Maybe all of them when combined together?

Who you really are? And why do you exist?

Are you one from the crowd of millions, who are just engaged
in the so-called happy & social life of our society and world?

Or are you The one in the crowd of millions, who is
strong enough to overcome all these distractions and hoax
of our moral and ethical society, that were made to
blind us from the reality & distract us from thinking,
exploring, interpreting and questioning the actual bigger picture.

The one who would like to follow
the path of the true knowledge.

The path to find the truth..
The path that other refers as madness.
The path which was only followed by few

Few personalities like Albert Einstein, like Nikola Tesla ,
like Issac Newton, like Galileo Galilei.

Yes I have looked at the night sky and wondered it
when i was a child...

Perhaps becoz a child is the only one, whose mind is
free enough of these distractions & emotions, while
his soul isn't enough tainted yet, that he can
clearly see through these dense mist of lies, which makes
him curious and causes him to wonder, the possibilities and the
vastness of the unknown, before he is consumed by the evil
temptations and living style of the world.

This brings us to our final question...

Have you ever wondered any of these facts?
No? then are you sure you've ever looked at the night sky?
Yes? but a long time ago..?

Then just stop reading reading my blog and today for real,
try to watch the night sky, full of stars,
free of citylights & clouds.

Believe me you automatically will feel some strange
sense of curiosity & attachment.

"So have you ever watched the Night Sky & Wondered?"

Signing off.
